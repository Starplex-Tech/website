What is Starplex?



Starplex is a local, technology producing organization, operating within Plain City, Ohio.

Starplex is newly founded with three core values in mind: Innovation, Service, and Expression!

1. Starplex aims to offer you with innovative yet simple technology.

2. Starplex aims to provide exceptional customer service. Not just through communication, but through prioritizing long-term strategy over short-term profit.

3. Starplex knows that there's no joy in doing anything without having some fun and expression! Embracing everything with pride, Starplex products aim to stand out with special quirks all around.
